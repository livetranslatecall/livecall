🔐 Privacy Policy

Clear information about how we handle your data — simple and transparent.

Effective: January 1, 2026

👤 Who processes your data?

LiveTranslate Call is a video calling application with real-time translation. The application is operated by an individual developer, not a large corporation.

📍 Data Controller

Data Controller: LiveTranslate Call (individual developer)
Contact: GitHub Issues
Website: livetranslatecall.github.io/livecall

📦 What data do we store?

We only store what is necessary for the service to function. No registration, no email address, no password.

Data type What we store Retention
Call data Room code, start/end time, participant count 5 days
Participant data Display name, selected language, join time 5 days
Chat messages Text messages (stored encrypted) 5 days
Network quality RTT, jitter, MOS values (anonymous) 5 days
Translation feedback 👍/👎 ratings (without personal data) 5 days
✅ What we DO NOT store

We do not store video or audio recordings, IP addresses, device identifiers, your real name, email address, or any payment information.

🗑️ Automatic deletion

All data is automatically deleted 5 days after the call. You don't need to do anything — it happens automatically.

⚠️ Important note

If you wish to export your call data (chat history, transcript), you must do so within 5 days after the call. After that, the data is irreversibly deleted.

🌍 Where do we store data?

📍 EU data storage

All data is stored within the European Union on Supabase EU West servers (Frankfurt, Germany). Data is not transferred outside the EU.

Video and audio data flows directly between the two participants via LiveKit servers and is not stored long-term.

🔒 Encryption and security

  • Chat messages: end-to-end encrypted (AES-GCM 256-bit E2EE)
  • Data transmission: TLS 1.3 encryption on all connections
  • Video and audio: server-side encryption on LiveKit infrastructure
  • Database: Supabase Row Level Security protection

⚖️ Your rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

👁️
Right of access
View the data stored for your calls
📥
Data portability
Export your data in JSON format
🗑️
Right to erasure
Delete all your data immediately
✋
Right to object
Object to the processing of your data
🔐 How to exercise your rights?

Click the "Data" button on the call screen to instantly export or delete your data. No separate request or waiting time is required.

🍪 Cookies

The application uses minimal local storage in your browser:

  • Translation cache — local storage of already translated texts for faster display
  • User preferences — selected language and display settings

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics tools.
